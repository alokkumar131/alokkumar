I'm Joyce Sueko

I am a multidisciplinary designer that create designs that are functional, meaningful and beautiful in Sydney.

For 4 years of my experience, I've been developing new products and services, being able to work from research to production. In my experience, I was able to design interactions for different touch points from web to mobile. I am self-aware of the impact our solutions have on people's lives, and this is the reason why I always give my best to create a seamless and delightful experience.
